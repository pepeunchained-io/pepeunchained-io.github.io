3.Liquidity Provision and Pool Dynamics:
Pepe Unchained relies on liquidity providers (LPs) to supply tokens to liquidity pools. In return for providing liquidity, LPs earn a share of the trading fees generated by the protocol. The dynamic nature of liquidity pools ensures that token prices adjust automatically based on supply and demand, providing a continuous and efficient trading environment.
